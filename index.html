About Us

Virtue Foundation

Virtue Foundation, initially registered as "Vailankanni Society for Rural Construction and Technical Education" on February 18, 1993, in Thiruvarur District under the Tamil Nadu Societies Registration Act, has been dedicated to welfare and development activities for over 30 years, focusing on women's savings, credit sangam, and employment opportunities for the downtrodden, handicapped, and marginalized sectors. The organization, also known as Virtue NGO, Virtue Mission, and Virtue Foundation for ease of reference, receives funding from the State and Central Government of India, as well as some support from abroad, to continue its mission of empowering vulnerable communities.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower People with disability and underprivileged people through developmental initiatives focusing on educational, social, economic, cultural and technological aspects.

Our Vision

To Empower Persons with Disability, poor and marginalized enabling them to live their life with dignity and respect.

Activities

Virtue Foundation enhances lives through education, health, employment, and livelihood initiatives, empowering marginalized communities.

Between 2004 and 2006 we run the SSA-programmer for the school drop outs with the supports education Officer (DEO of Thiruvarur District). Through TANSACS Chennai we have running Community care for the HIV positive persons in Thiruvarur District.

Intervention with People with Disabilities (PwD)

VIRTUE has organized many awareness programme on PwD Act to rural community and it has women SHG federation. So far from 2014, VIRTUE organized 75 awareness programme on PwD Acts. The awareness enhanced to promote inclusion of PwDs in any programmes and SHGs. And also enhanced to promote exclusively PwD SHGs.

VIRTUE strength is that having 400 women SHGs and one Women SHG Federation in Thriuthuraipoondi block of Thirvarur district. Due to multiple interventions, 60 women SHGs have included PwDs and in 15 Women SHGs, PwDs are leaders in the inclusive SHGs.

Apart from inclusion, VIRTUE has organized exclusively for SHG for PwDs where five to six PwDs are members in the SHGs and the PwDs SHGs were trained on various concepts like Account and book keeping, PwD Acts, Government Schemes for PwDs, etc. VIRTUE is having 30 PwDs SHGs.

With support of NIEPMD- SIPDA, VIRTUE organized coir winding training to 30 PwDs for FIVE Months. Moreover technical training, It also exposed procurement, marketing, calculating cost of production, etc. Finally, VIRTUE distributed certificates to participants. All PwDs participants were from VIRTUE PwDs.

To build awareness of disability issues, the VIRTUE conducts workshops and presentations to inform and connect with society. Midwives, panchyat officers (community leaders), police, Government officials are trained to ensure early recognition and fair treatment of those with disabilities. Organization also organized awareness on Government schemes for parents and guardians.

VIRTUE has organized one day capacity building programme for PwDs SHGs on PwD Act and disabled rights. All VIRTUE SHGs were participated the one day programme and aware of their rights.

Worth of 30 lakh of Relief materials were distributed to victims of Gaja cyclone exclusively for multiple disability PwDs. The relief maintains all basic stuffs for kitchen which were supported by ONGC. Apart from kitchen kits, Agricultural tools were distributed to the families of multiple disability PwDs.

The Model design for Day and Night Shelters is a prototype design for minimum 50 beds for the homeless. The shelters have accommodation for - Males, Females, Family and Disabled. The Designs are based on a spatial configuration and layout which is suited to the four main climatic zones of India: i. Hot and Dry climate ii. Composite climate iii. Warm and Humid climate iv. Target Intervention with Commercial Sex Workers

The targeted intervention programme amongst the Female Sex Workers in Thiruvarur was organized. This programme is funded by the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation through Voluntary Health Services (VHS) Chennai. Organized. Thiruvarur areas are the areas where VIRTUE NGO has its intervention activities among these target community. This is a community driven prevention programme for the female commercial sex workers in the identified region in the vulnerable pockets of the Madurai city. A special Clinic cum Laboratory with Counseling center is been in operation in the region with qualified and dedicated team of Medical, Para medical and Experienced staff. Periodical review and regular referral services with one to one counseling activities are some of our specialised services for the benefit of the target community.

Special Shelter for people attending patient

organize shelter for people attending patient at Government Hospital at Thirvarur. The Shelter has hygienic bathrooms and toilets, fully furnished dromedary, clock rooms, etc. The programme will be initiated on April 1st week of 2019.

Survey on Bagger community and Street vendors

VIRTUE has organized survey on baggers and street vendors in Nagapattinam and Pondicherry districts. It has found that 50 baggers in Nagapattinam district and 504 baggers in Pondichery district. There are 1000 street vendors in Nagapattinam district. 4500 street vendors in Pondychery District.

Intervention with HIV/AIDS

With support of TANSC, We organized community care center for Persons living with HIV/AIDS often find that their family and community connections are shattered by the illness and the fear and stigma that accompany it; the center guiding principle has been to restore a sense of solidarity between people living with HIV/AIDS and their community. Encouraging the direct involvement of PLHIV in counseling, prevention, support, fund-raising, income generation and care are all activities that directly alleviate the immediate situation.
